Soup Arzu

Description

Ingredients

  • Onion, 2 and a half.
  • Chicken Stock, 1 liter.
  • Flour, 1 cup.
  • Olive Oil, 4 tbsp.
  • Butter, 2 tbsp.
  • Salt 2 tsp.
  • Pepper, to taste.
  • Sugar, 1/2 tsp.
  • White Wine, 1 cup.
  • Bay Leaf, 1.
  • Bread, 1 loaf.
  • Swiss Cheese, shredded, 1 cup.

Steps

  1. Heat the oil and butter in a pot, then throw in the onion.
  2. Leave fire on medium for 20 minutes.
  3. When the onion is translucent, pour in the flour.
  4. Let the flour incorporate with the onion and become sludgy.
  5. Pour in one cup of the chicken stock and mix with the flour and onions.
  6. Pour in the rest of the stock, as well as the white wine.
  7. Drop the bay leaf then let simmer for 40 minutes while you prepare the bread.
  8. Butter the bread, then put it in the oven for 30 mins. at 325Â° F.
  9. After simmering, salt and pepper the soup, add the cheese.
  10. Serve the soup with a piece of bread.
  11. Enjoy!
